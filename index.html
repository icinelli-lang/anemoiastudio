PROJECT

CAIRO x UTMB

Cairo – Egypt Ultra-Trail by UTMB is a brand identity project created for an ultra-trail race in the heart of the Egyptian desert. The visual identity blends the spirit of trail running with the iconic desert landscape, transforming dunes, mountains, and movement into a bold and recognizable graphic system. The result is a brand that conveys adventure, endurance, and exploration through a modern visual language.

Dual - Brooks

Dual is a concept project developed for Brooks, including footwear design, an advertising campaign, and a dedicated landing page. Built around the idea of duality in performance, the project explores the balance between speed and comfort through a cohesive visual identity and product experience. The result is a bold, contemporary concept that connects innovation, storytelling, and digital communication into one unified brand experience.

OFFFY

Offfy is a mobile app concept designed to help high school students reduce digital dopamine dependency and build healthier screen habits. Through challenges, rewards, and real-world experiences, the app encourages users to spend less time on their phones and engage more with local events and offline activities. The project combines UX design, gamification, and visual identity to promote a more balanced and mindful digital lifestyle.

Play Pulse

Play Pulse is a team-building service designed for companies, using VR technology to create immersive challenges that strengthen focus, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. Through interactive experiences and concentration-based activities, the project transforms traditional corporate training into an engaging and memorable experience. The result is a modern brand that combines innovation, technology, and human connection.

Reloop

ReLoop is a mobile app concept designed to promote recycling and the circular economy by turning citizens into active participants in waste management. Using a smart scanning system, the app identifies and maps recyclable waste while rewarding users with points redeemable at partner stores. The project combines UX design, gamification, and sustainability to create an engaging ecosystem that encourages responsible habits and gives waste a second life.
